About Me

Jesutofunmi “Tofunmi” Oyeleke is a student at the University of Indianapolis, pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics as part of the class of 2026.

He is actively involved with the UIndy Speech Team and DesignSpine, and has strong expertise in frontend, backend, and full-stack development, along with experience in product management.

He is passionate about leadership, mentorship, and building communities that empower students like himself to thrive.

Outside of academics and tech, he enjoys sports (with the exception of baseball), especially playing tennis, running, and reading biographies.
